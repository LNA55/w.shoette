Correlation is not causation. W is a tool for surfacing correlations: it asserts no causation and makes no diagnosis.

How it works

From scattered symptoms to a pattern.

Three steps. No forms, no medical jargon, no effort.

01

Talk

Describe the pain in your own words — where it is, how strong, when it changes — plus sleep, food, stress, the weather, what you took and when. Photograph a scan or a lab result.

02

Connect

Link the apps you already use — sleep, steps, weather, calendar, cycle, any health app. It pulls in hundreds of signals a day and structures them cleanly, in any language.

03

Understand

It weighs everything at once against medical knowledge and your own history, surfacing the multi-factor patterns behind your pain — and citing the sources it relied on.

Security & compliance

Your health data, under lock and law.

Health data is the most sensitive data there is — a "special category" under the GDPR (Art. 9). We treat it that way: built for strict compliance with European and French law, by design, and engineered to go further than the law requires.

Collection — minimised & consented

We collect only what a finding actually needs, and pseudonymise it the moment it arrives. Analysis runs on de-identified data — and only ever with your explicit, opt-in consent, which you can withdraw at any time.

GDPR Art. 9Art. 5(1)(c)Art. 7

Storage — you decide how long

Your entries are kept for you to use, then — after three years — we tell you a month ahead and let you keep or delete them. Or opt in, once, to keep everything for as long as you want it.

GDPR Art. 5(1)(e)Storage limitation

Transmission — encrypted end to end

Every byte moves over TLS 1.3 and rests under AES-256. Pseudonymisation and encryption are applied by default — exactly the technical measures the law names for securing processing.

GDPR Art. 32TLS 1.3AES-256

Encryption & keys — separated, rotated

AES-256-GCM at rest, with per-user keys held apart from the data they protect and rotated on a schedule. Confidentiality, integrity, availability and resilience are tested continuously.

GDPR Art. 32Key isolation

Where it lives — certified EU data centres

Your data stays in the EU, on infrastructure built to the HDS standard (Hébergeur de Données de Santé) required for hosting French health data — an ISO 27001-based framework overseen by the Agence du Numérique en Santé.

Code de la santé publique L.1111-8HDSISO 27001

Your rights — always yours

Access, correct, export in a portable format (any date range, your physical, psychological and context data), or erase everything — one tap, honoured without undue delay. And we run a Data Protection Impact Assessment before we ever launch.

GDPR Art. 15–20Art. 17Art. 35

Built to comply with the GDPR (Regulation (EU) 2016/679) and France's Loi Informatique et Libertés — and to go further. Your data is never sold, never used to train anyone else's AI, and never shared without your explicit say-so. Privacy isn't a setting here — it's the architecture.
